Compass

Find Your Direction. Follow Your Curiosity.

Compass is a gifted learning platform for middle school students. Explore strengths, set goals, create meaningful projects, build a digital portfolio, earn Passport stamps, and share teacher-approved work in the Compass Showcase.

Explore

Discover interests, strengths, and areas you want to pursue deeply.

Create

Use Challenge Cards and the COMPASS process to build authentic projects.

Reflect

Track gifted goals, grades, growth, and teacher feedback.

Showcase

Build a private portfolio and submit selected work for teacher approval.
